The Porsche Taycan is Porsche’s first all-electric car, offering sharp handling, luxury, and high performance.
Models like the Turbo S deliver up to 750 hp and 0–60 mph in 2.4 seconds.
It features ultra-fast 800-volt charging and a 200–300 mile range, plus a tech-rich interior with up to four screens.
